## How does Revroute Partners work?

Revroute Partners is an all-in-one partner management platform for you to scale your revenue by incentivizing both your users and external partners to help spread the word about your product.

1. **You create a program** – Set up your partner program, define reward rules (e.g., % of sale, fixed amount per lead), and configure payouts.
2. **Partners join and get links** – Partners apply or are invited, then receive unique tracking links (short links or query-parameter links).
3. **Visitors click and convert** – When someone clicks a partner link and later signs up or buys, Revroute attributes that conversion to the partner.
4. **Commissions are calculated** – Revroute applies your reward rules and creates commissions.
5. **Partners get paid** – You can pay via Revroute (Stripe Connect) or handle payouts externally.

Revroute Partners is built on top of our [enterprise-grade attribution infrastructure](https://revroute.ru/) that powers 100M+ click and conversion events monthly.

## Key concepts

| Concept           | What it means                                                                                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Program**       | Your partner program (e.g., "Acme Affiliate Program"). Each workspace can have one program.                      |
| **Partner**       | A person or company in your program who shares links and earns commissions.                                      |
| **Partner group** | A tier or segment of partners (e.g., "Affiliates", "VIP Partners") with its own reward rules and link structure. |
| **Reward rules**  | How partners earn: per click, per lead, per sale, or a mix. You define these per group.                          |
| **Conversion**    | A tracked action (lead or sale) that gets attributed to a partner link.                                          |
| **Commission**    | The amount a partner earns from a conversion, based on your reward rules.                                        |

### Flexible reward structures

With Revroute, you can create advanced pay-per-click/lead and rev-share reward structures to drive partner engagement and revenue.

| Reward type                     | Description                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Click-based (pay-per-click)** | Rewarding partners for driving clicks to your product – similar to how Google Ads' pay-per-click works.                                            |
| **Lead-based (pay-per-lead)**   | A more effective alternative to pay-per-click, where you reward partners for leads generated (e.g. demo requests, free trial signups, etc.)        |
| **Sale-based (pay-per-sale)**   | This is the most common reward type, where you reward partners for driving sales to your product either via a rev-share or fixed-price commission. |

Learn more about [how to create partner rewards](/help/managing-a-program/rewards-and-commissions/partner-rewards) on Revroute Partners.
