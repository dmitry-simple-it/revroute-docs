If you're not sure where a Revroute link will take you, you can check its destination before clicking on it by adding a `+` sign to the end of the short link.

For example, if you want to check the destination of the short link `d.to/github`, you can type `d.to/github+` into your browser's address bar and press `Enter`.

![Inspect a Revroute link](https://assets.dub.co/changelog/link-inspector.png)

This will take you to a page that shows you the destination URL of the short link, as well as the respective social media preview for the URL.

## Reporting a malicious link

If you suspect that a Revroute link is malicious, you can report it by clicking on the `🚩` button on the link inspector page.

![Reporting a malicious link on Revroute](https://assets.dub.co/help/report-link.png)

This will open up a form where you can provide more information about the malicious link.

![Reporting a malicious link on Revroute](https://assets.dub.co/help/report-link-form.png)

We take these reports very seriously and will review and take appropriate action as soon as possible.

Learn more about the types of links that are against our [Terms of Service](https://revroute.ru/legal/terms#3-fair-use).
