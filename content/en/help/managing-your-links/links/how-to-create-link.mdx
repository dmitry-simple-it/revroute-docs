Revroute's link builder comes with powerful features like [UTM builder](/help/managing-your-links/links/utm-builder), [device targeting](/help/managing-your-links/links/device-targeting), [password protection](/help/managing-your-links/links/password-protected-links), [expiration dates](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-expiration), and more.

Revroute is also the only link management platform that lets you [customize the social media cards](/help/article/custom-link-previews) for your links, so you can have full control over how your links look when shared on social media.

In this article, we will take a look at how you can leverage these features to create your first short link on Revroute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Revroute link builder">
    
    There are 3 ways to open the [Revroute link builder](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/dub-link-builder) to create a short link:

    1. **Slowest**: Click on the "Create link" button on the dashboard.
    2. **Faster**: Use the keyboard shortcut `c` to open the link builder.
    3. **Fastest**: Just paste in a valid URL into the dashboard. This will automatically open the link builder and pre-fill the URL field.

    ![The Revroute link builder](https://assets.dub.co/help/dub-link-builder.jpg)

    As you can see, the link builder also gives you a preview of your short link on social media platforms like X (formerly Twitter), Facebook, and LinkedIn.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your destination URL">
    Enter your destination URL in the "Destination URL" field. You can enter the URL with or without the `https://` protocol – behind the scenes, Revroute will automatically make sure it's formatted correctly.
    
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a short link slug" optional>
    Under the **Short Link** field, you can either:
    1. Enter your own short link slug
    2. Generate a random short link slug
    3. [Generate a short link slug with AI](https://dub.co/blog/introducing-dub-ai).

    <video src="https://assets.dub.co/help/generate-shortlink-ai.mp4"
        loop
        autoPlay
        muted
        playsInline
      ></video>

    <Tip>
      This step is optional. If you don't enter a short link slug, Revroute will generate one for you.
    </Tip>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize your link previews" optional>
    You can also [set custom link previews](/help/article/custom-link-previews) for your short link to control how your links look when shared on social media platforms like X (formerly Twitter), LinkedIn, Slack, Telegram, iMessage, and more.

    ![Create custom link previews for your links](https://assets.dub.co/help/custom-link-previews.png)

    You can also use AI to generate custom link previews for your links. [Learn more](/help/article/custom-link-previews).

    <video src="https://assets.dub.co/help/generate-metatags-ai.mp4"
        loop
        autoPlay
        muted
        playsInline
      ></video>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add tags to your short link" optional>
    Optionally, you can add tags to your short link to help organize your links and make them easier to find.

    ![Fun fact: You can assign multiple tags to a given Revroute link](https://assets.dub.co/help/multiple-tags-dropdown.png)

    You can also [filter your analytics by tags](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/filtering-analytics/filter-analytics-by-tags), which allows you to create powerful campaign-specific reports for your links.

  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it! You've just created your first short link on Revroute.

Additionally, Revroute also offers a variety of advanced link features that you can use to enhance your short links:

1. **UTM Builder**: Add UTM parameters to your links. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/utm-builder).
2. **Link Cloaking**: Mask your destination URL with your short link. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-cloaking).
3. **Password Protection**: Protect your links with a password. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/password-protected-links).
4. **Expiration Date**: Set an expiration date for your links. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-expiration).
5. **Device Targeting**: Set a custom destination URL for iOS and Android devices. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/device-targeting).
6. **Geo Targeting**: Redirect your users to different links based on their location. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/geo-targeting).
7. **Search Engine Indexing**: Control how your links are indexed by search engines. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/how-noindex-works).
8. **Comments**: Add comments to your links – for you and your team. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-comments).
