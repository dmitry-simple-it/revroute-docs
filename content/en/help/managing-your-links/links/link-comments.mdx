---
title: "How to add comments to your links in Revroute?"
description: "Learn how to add comments to your links in Revroute to provide more context."
sidebarTitle: "Link comments"
---

On Revroute, you can add comments to your links by using the "Comments" section in the link builder.

![Adding comments to your links in the Revroute link builder](https://assets.dub.co/help/comments-section.png)

<Info>
  Note: Hyperlinks are supported in comments. You can use this to add more
  context to your links.
</Info>

When you add a comment to your link, it will be visible to you and your team as a
message bubble on the link card.

![Comments are visible as message bubbles on the link card](https://assets.dub.co/changelog/link-comments.png)
