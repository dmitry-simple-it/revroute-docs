First of all, if you are planning to cancel your subscription, we're really sorry to see you go! We would appreciate any feedback on how we could improve our platform – feel free to [send us an email](mailto:support@revroute.ru?subject=Feedback%20About%20Dub) or [DM us on Twitter](https://twitter.com/dubdotco)!

## How can I cancel my Revroute subscription?

You can cancel your Revroute subscription by following the steps below:

1. From your workspace dashboard (e.g. `app.revroute.ru/your-workspace`), click on the **Settings** tab.
2. On the left sidebar, click on **Billing**.
3. Click on the **Cancel subscription** button to cancel your subscription.

![Billing section on Revroute](https://assets.dub.co/cms/billing-subscription.png)

We really hope to see you back someday!

## How can I downgrade my Revroute subscription?

If you would like to downgrade your subscription instead, please follow these steps:

1. From your workspace dashboard (e.g. `app.revroute.ru/your-workspace`), click on the **Settings** tab.
2. On the left sidebar, click on **Billing**.
3. Click on the **Manage plan** button to see all available plans on Revroute.

![Billing section on Revroute](https://assets.dub.co/cms/billing-subscription.png)

4. Select the plan that works best for you. You can also click on the **Adjust usage** button and choose your preferred link or event usage level.

![](https://assets.dub.co/cms/adjust usage button.png)

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please [contact our support team](https://revroute.ru//support).
