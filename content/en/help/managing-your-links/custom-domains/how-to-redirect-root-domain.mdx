<Tip>
  This feature is only available on [Pro plans and
  above](https://revroute.ru/pricing).
</Tip>

When you add a custom domain to your workspace, Revroute will automatically create a placeholder page for your domain. This page will be displayed when you visit your domain.

![Placeholder page for a custom domain on Revroute](https://assets.dub.co/help/dub-placeholder-page.png)

To redirect your domain to a URL of your choice, follow these steps:

1. If you haven't already, upgrade to the [Pro plan](https://dub.co/pricing).
2. Navigate to your workspace's Domains page – you can do that by clicking on the **Domains** link in the menu bar at the top of your workspace dashboard.
3. Click on the `⋮` button next to the domain you want to redirect, which will open a dropdown menu.

![More options popover](https://assets.dub.co/help/domain-popover-menu.png)

4. In the dropdown menu, click on the **Edit Link** button, which will open the [Revroute link builder](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/dub-link-builder) for the root domain link.
5. Enter the URL you want to redirect your domain to in the **Destination URL** field.

![The ](https://assets.dub.co/help/edit-domain-link.png)

6. Click "Save changes". Your root domain link will now redirect to the URL you just specified.

## Advanced link features

Additionally, Revroute also offers a variety of advanced link features that you can use to enhance your root domain links:

1. **Custom Link Previews**: Customize how your links show up on social media to improve click-through rates. [Learn more](/help/article/custom-link-previews).
2. **Tags**: Assign tags to your links to organize them. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/how-to-use-tags).
3. **UTM Builder**: Add UTM parameters to your links. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/utm-builder).
4. **Link Cloaking**: Mask your destination URL with your short link. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-cloaking).
5. **Password Protection**: Protect your links with a password. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/password-protected-links).
6. **Expiration Date**: Set an expiration date for your links. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-expiration).
7. **Device Targeting**: Set a custom destination URL for iOS and Android devices. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/device-targeting).
8. **Geo Targeting**: Redirect your users to different links based on their location. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/geo-targeting).
9. **Search Engine Indexing**: Control how your links are indexed by search engines. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/how-noindex-works).
10. **Comments**: Add comments to your links – for you and your team. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-comments).
