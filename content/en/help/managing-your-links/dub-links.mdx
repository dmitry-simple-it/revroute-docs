Revroute Links is the modern link management platform for modern marketing teams.

With Revroute, you can [create short links](/help/managing-your-links/links/how-to-create-link) using your own [custom domain](/help/managing-your-links/custom-domains/how-to-add-custom-domain) and get [world-class analytics](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/dub-analytics) on your links' performance.


## What can I do with Revroute Links?

If you are a marketer or a business owner, Revroute has the perfect suite of features for you to better manage your marketing campaigns with short links:

<Steps>

<Step title="Powerful link builder">

![The Revroute link builder](https://assets.dub.co/help/dub-link-builder.jpg)

Our [link builder](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/dub-link-builder) comes with features like:

- [UTM builder](/help/managing-your-links/links/utm-builder)
- [Custom link previews](/help/article/custom-link-previews)
- [Device targeting](/help/managing-your-links/links/device-targeting)
- [Geo targeting](/help/managing-your-links/links/geo-targeting)
- [Password protection](/help/managing-your-links/links/password-protected-links)
- [Expiration dates](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-expiration)
- [Link cloaking](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-cloaking)

</Step>

<Step title="Use your own domain">

On Revroute, you can create short links with your own [custom domain](/help/managing-your-links/custom-domains/how-to-add-custom-domain) – for better brand recognition.

We also have one of the most generous custom domain limits in the industry – you can add up to 3 custom domains to your workspace for free, and even more with our [paid plans](https://revroute.ru/pricing).

- [**Pro plan**](https://revroute.ru/pricing): 10 domains
- [**Business plan**](https://revroute.ru/pricing): 40 domains
- [**Enterprise plan**](https://revroute.ru/): Unlimited domains

</Step>

<Step title="Analytics that matter">

We provide you with [powerful analytics](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/dub-analytics) for your links, including geolocation, device, browser, and referrer information.

- [Real-time events stream](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/real-time-events-stream)
- [Exporting your analytics data](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/how-to-export-analytics)
- ["Ask AI" feature](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/dub-analytics#bonus-ask-ai-feature)

</Step>

<Step title="Free QR code generator">

QR codes and short links are like peas in a pod. That's why we've built a QR code generator right into Revroute that lets you generate branded QR codes for your links.

</Step>

<Step title="Team collaboration">

Revroute is built for teams. You can [invite your teammates](/help/article/how-to-invite-teammates) to your account for free and manage your links together.

On Revroute [Enterprise](https://revroute.ru/), you can also set up [SAML/SSO and SCIM Directory Sync](/help/article/okta-saml) for greater security and control.

</Step>

<Step title="Enterprise-grade API">

If you're planning to use Revroute to create links programmatically, we have a powerful, [enterprise-grade API](/docs/api-reference/introduction) that allows you to do just that.

We also offer native SDKs for:

- TypeScript
- Python
- Go
- Ruby

Our API is used in production by engineering teams at companies like Raycast, [Whop](https://whop.com), and Midday.

</Step>

</Steps>
