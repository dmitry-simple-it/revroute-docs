<Tip>
  This feature is only available on [Revroute Enterprise](https://dub.co/enterprise).
</Tip>

For Revroute Enterprise users, you can automatically provision and deprovision users from your Azure Active Directory (AD) to Revroute using [SCIM Directory Sync](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/active-directory/architecture/sync-scim).

<CheckList>
  <CheckListItem
    title="Create a SAML application in Azure"
    href="/help/article/azure-saml"
  >
    Before you can configure SCIM Directory Sync, you need to create a SAML
    application in Azure.
  </CheckListItem>
</CheckList>

## Step 1: Configure Directory Sync on Revroute

In your workspace dashboard on Revroute, click on the **Settings** tab in the menu bar at the top. Then, click on the **Security** tab in the sidebar.

![Directory Sync section on the Revroute Dashboard](https://assets.dub.co/cms/saml-directory-sync-2.png)

Under the **Directory Sync** section, click on **Configure**. This will open up the Directory Sync modal:

1. Select **Azure AD** as the Directory Provider.
2. Click **Save changes**.

![SCIM Modal](https://assets.dub.co/cms/scim-entra.png)

This will generate a Directory Sync connection for your Revroute workspace, and return 2 values, which will be needed in Step 2:

1. **Tenant URL**
2. **Secret Token**

![SCIM Modal](https://assets.dub.co/cms/scim-entra-confirm.png)

## Step 2: Add Provisioning to SAML Application

Click on the **Provisioning** tab of your existing Revroute Okta SAML application that you want to enable SCIM provisioning for.

![Provisioning tab of Okta SAML application](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-tab.png)

In the Provisioning tab, click on **Get started**.

![Get started with provisioning](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-get-started.png)

Select **Automatic** from the **Provisioning Mode** dropdown. Under the **Admin Credentials** section, enter the values that you obtained from Step 1:

1. **Tenant URL**
2. **Secret Token**

![Provisioning tab of Azure SAML application](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-provisioning.png)

Click on **Test Connection** to test the connection to see if the credentials are correct, then click **Save** to save the credentials.

Expand the **Mappings** section and ensure group and user attribute mappings are enabled for your app. The default mapping should work.

![Mappings section of Azure SAML application](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-mappings.png)

Expand the **Settings** section and make the following changes:

- Select **Sync only assigned users and groups** from the **Scope** dropdown.
- Confirm the **Provisioning Status** is set to **On**.

Click **Save** to save the changes.

![Settings section of Azure SAML application](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-settings.png)

Congratulations! You've successfully configured SCIM provisioning for your Revroute workspace.

## Step 3: Assign Users

Once you've configured Directory sync, you can assign users to Revroute directly within Azure AD.

From your application, click the **Users and groups** from the left navigation menu and click **Add user/group**.

![Adding users in Azure AD](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-add-users.png)

Click on **None Selected** under **Users**.

From the right side of the screen, select the users you want to assign to the app and click the **Select** button. Thenm click **Assign** to those users to your app.

![Assigning users in Azure AD](https://assets.dub.co/help/azure-scim-assign-user.png)

Your assigned users should now receive an invitation email to join your Revroute workspace.

![SAML invite email](https://assets.dub.co/help/saml-invite.png)

<Warning>
  Azure AD SCIM provisioning can take [anywhere between 20-40 minutes to
  sync](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/databricks/administration-guide/users-groups/scim/aad#provisioning-tips).
  This means that it may take up to 40 minutes for your users to receive the
  invitation email and be able to join your Revroute workspace.
</Warning>

They will also be able to sign in to Revroute using Azure AD SSO
