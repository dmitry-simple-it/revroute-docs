Short links are the bread and butter of Dub. With Dub's link management platform, you can create short links and [QR codes](/help/managing-your-links/links/custom-qr-codes) for your marketing campaigns and get [world-class analytics](/help/managing-your-links/analytics/dub-analytics) on them.

At the center of Dub is the Dub link builder. This is where you'll find all the tools & features you need to create a short link on Dub.

![The Dub link builder](https://assets.dub.co/help/dub-link-builder.jpg)

## Features of the Dub link builder

On the left half of the link builder, Dub offers a variety of advanced link features that you can use to create and manage your short links.

1. **Tag selector**: Organize your links with tags to make them easy to find. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/how-to-use-tags).
2. **Custom link previews**: Customize how your link appears on social media with the help of AI to improve conversion rates [Learn more](/help/article/custom-link-previews).
3. **UTM Builder**: Add UTM parameters to your links. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/utm-builder).
4. **Link Cloaking**: Mask your destination URL with your short link. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-cloaking).
5. **Password Protection**: Protect your links with a password. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/password-protected-links).
6. **Expiration Date**: Set an expiration date for your links. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-expiration).
7. **Device Targeting**: Set a custom destination URL for iOS and Android devices. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/device-targeting).
8. **Geo Targeting**: Redirect your users to different links based on their location. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/geo-targeting).
9. **Search Engine Indexing**: Control how your links are indexed by search engines. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/how-noindex-works).
10. **Comments**: Add comments to your links – for you and your team. [Learn more](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-comments).

On the right half of the link builder, you get a live preview of how your short link will look on social media platforms like **X (formerly Twitter)**, **Facebook**, and **LinkedIn**.

## Keyboard shortcuts in the Dub link builder

The Dub link builder also comes with a variety of keyboard shortcuts to help you create links faster. Here's a list of all the keyboard shortcuts you can use:

| Shortcut | Action                                                                 |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `D`      | Open the domain selector                                               |
| `T`      | Open the tag selector                                                  |
| `Q`      | Open the QR code modal                                                 |
| `L`      | Open the [link preview](/help/article/custom-link-previews) modal      |
| `U`      | Open the [UTM builder](/help/managing-your-links/links/utm-builder)                      |
| `E`      | Open the [link expiration](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-expiration) modal        |
| `G`      | Open the [geo/device targeting](/help/managing-your-links/links/geo-targeting) modal     |
| `P`      | Open the [link password](/help/managing-your-links/links/password-protected-links) modal |
| `K`      | Toggle [link cloaking](/help/managing-your-links/links/link-cloaking)                    |
| `S`      | Toggle [search engine indexing](/help/managing-your-links/additional-guides/how-noindex-works)       |
| `A`      | Open the advanced settings modal                                       |

## How to open the Dub link builder

There are 3 ways to open the Dub link builder:

- 🐢 **Slowest**: Click on the "Create link" button on the dashboard.
- 🐇 **Faster**: Use the keyboard shortcut `c` to open the link builder.
- 🐅 **Fastest**: Just paste in a valid URL into the dashboard. This will automatically open the link builder and pre-fill the URL field.

## Link creation shortcuts on Dub

1. Enter `dub.new` in your browser. This will open the link builder in your [default Dub workspace](/help/managing-your-workspace/additional-guides/how-to-change-default-workspace).
